Trust Alliance scheme

2017-05-18

By means of Trust Alliance scheme, as long as users keep exchanging a public key that is necessary to encrypt a set of data for Cinematic Communication including character data with only partners that they can trust, they can also technically respect the right of character data that creators made.

Heartfelt and lively communication with only reliable partners matches with the features of Cinematic Communication and it sounds logical for most of them.

For creators as long as characters that they made are used among reliable users, they do not need to worry too much about illegal distribution of the pirated data.
