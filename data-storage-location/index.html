Data storage location

2017-05-18

It is necessary to use online storage service in order to save and manage character data and exchanged message data for Cinematic Communication.

For example, a procedure to save character data purchased from distributors is as follows.

  1. At first you purchase character data from one distributer and save the data in your personal computer or mobile device after downloading it.

  2. Then you prepare online storage in advance and upload the data to it.

  3. If the capacity of memory for saving the data in your computer or mobile device is limited, you can delete the data after uploading it to the online storage. However, if you wish to enjoy Cinematic Communication even while offline, you do not need to delete it.

  4. For example, if you download the character data in your computer, upload it to the online storage at first and then copy it to your mobile device directly, you can enjoy Cinematic Communication on your mobile device even while offline.

  5. If you wish to enjoy Cinematic Communication while offline, it is only possible when you have character data that is saved in your computer or mobile device. It is available to use character data that is owned by your friends or family members registered on your friend list only while online.
