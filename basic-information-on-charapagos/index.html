About charapagos

Charapagos is a system to produce Cinematic Communication that is a new type of online communication with making the best use of digital characters.

Demonstration

You can try the demonstration of Cinematic Communication from the below URL. Please do not forget to include emotional key words like “Happy” or “Sad” in your message. Digital characters will act according to the key words.

https://raw.githack.com/charapagos/interface/master/cp_demo_v2_2_3/character_selection.html

The following character named Abungu can show 36 emotional and actional expressions at this moment.

https://raw.githack.com/charapagos/interface/master/cp_demo_v2_2_3/index.html#abungu_800
