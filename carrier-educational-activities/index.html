Carrier educational activities

2017-05-18

We as developers for Cinematic Communication have a history to keep supporting carrier educational activities that aim to foster the next generation of creators and users cooperating with Japanese major animation studios for more than 10 years.

Every year around 60 to 100 sixth graders in an elementary school joined these educational activities and produced original short animation films at a group unit following professional animation production procedure.

The report for FY 2015 carrier educational activities with academic analysis is uploaded on the following URL.

https://carrier-educational-activities.github.io/

For your reference, 79 students tried these educational activities in FY 2016 spending more than 40 school hours. Each of 18 groups completed its original animation work.

“Pierrot Co.,Ltd.”, “STUDIO GHIBLI Inc.” and “Telecom Animation Film Co.,Ltd.” that represent the Japanese animation industry have been collaborating with these activities.
