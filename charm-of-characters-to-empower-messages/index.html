Charm of characters to empower messages

2017-05-18

Cinematic Communication expects characters for example ones in animation works to give more power to sentences and messages making the best use of their features including their appearance and personality.

You can tell more effectively about what you want to say to your partner only because of adding the feature of a specific character on sentences or messages in some cases.

If you wish to prompt a smile from your communication partner, there are more possibilities to do so through selecting a popper character appeared in a comedy work and sending encouraging words with it than only sentences or messages.

You can also try Cinematic Communication with a character that your partner loves. When your partner is disappointed, it is easy for you to encourage him/her with the action of his/her favorite character.
