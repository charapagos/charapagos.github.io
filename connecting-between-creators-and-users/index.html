Connecting between creators and users

2017-05-18

Cinematic Communication can shorten the distance between creators and users.

Through Cinematic Communication, users can feel more familiar with an excellent ability to express that professional creators possess.

For users, Cinematic Communication can provide them more opportunities to enjoy works made by creators.

For creators, shortening the distance with users through Cinematic Communication will be important more than before.

An animation industry has already reached the level to produce high quality works more or less than live action films through advanced division of labor system. On the other hand, it is separating itself from users who watch their works.

For example, we hear a sad story that creators who are making works for children do not know well about what kinds of works or visual expression children really wish to watch.

They can directly get more information on what kinds of characters users including children like or what kids of expression they chose.

They can also learn ideas about new characters or expression methods from users.
