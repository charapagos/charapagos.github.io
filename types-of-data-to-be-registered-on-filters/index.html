Types of data to be registered on filters

2017-05-18

Two types of data, character data and words or phrases related to expression data are basic to be registered on filters. However, you can register a variety of data without them.

For example, after you register temperature data from minus 20C until 50C in advance, you can combine a phrase that suggests more than 25C in a message with character data that express sweating. On the contrary when a phrase that suggests less than 10C is used, character data that express freezing can be combined with the phrase.

Without words or phrases, for example music data can be registered in a filter. We will realize to act characters along with pitches and scales in a song instead of messages from now on.
