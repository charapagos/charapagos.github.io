Emotional Engine

2017-05-18

Emotional Engine embedded in charapagos is the heart of Cinematic Communication and literally driving force to make characters express and act.

To be more precise, it has a function to combine emotional words or phrases in sentences that users wrote with characters’ expression or action data.

Combination between emotional words and characters’ expression or action data is up to users and everybody can do it easily.

Once they write sentences normally, Emotional Engine will automatically find emotional words in them and select appropriate characters’ expression or action data along with that.

If they wish to pick up other specific phrases or characters’ expression or action data, they are allowed to do so easily.

There is a common method to combine words with characters’ expression or action data that everybody can easily use or each user can prepare a special one.
