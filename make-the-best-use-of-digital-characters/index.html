Make the best use of digital characters

2017-05-18

Cinematic Communication is quite compatible with 3D animation characters. Of course you can also sufficiently enjoy with 2D ones. However, 3D ones are expected to extend the possibility of Cinematic Communication furthermore.

As compared with 2D characters originated in traditional animation, 3D ones are created by modeling the whole part of them from the beginning. After that it is easier to make them express and act as their feature. Cinematic Communication can follow the feature directly.

In other words, Cinematic Communication can smoothly make use of characters in 3D animation works for a second time that were distributed or broadcasted for business.

We think that it is very important for the whole animation industry to produce opportunities to reuse digitalized characters, increase the source of income for creators and improve environment where they can more dedicate themselves to creative activities.

For your information, the truth is that most of cell images and characters drawn on them for 2D animation works including former masterpieces are never reused and sleeping in dark stockyards.
