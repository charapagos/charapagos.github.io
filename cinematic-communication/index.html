Cinematic Communication enables you to enjoy more heartfelt and lively communication with your friends and family members with a feeling of film making as its name suggests.

Like a scriptwriter or choreographer you can move digital characters more dynamically.

It is very easy to enable digital characters to act. You do not need to buy dedicated software for the purpose or learn complicated operation procedures.

Only writing a sentence as usual is enough.

In so doing, the trick is to add words that tell your feeling considering your communication partners.

According to words that reflect your feeling, digital characters will start to move.

Nowadays the size and resolution of a screen not only for a TV but also mobile device is rapidly improving and Cinematic Communication can develop a new style of communication that is appropriate to the improvement.

A new world where digital characters are ready to move will spread in a screen with various sizes from a large liquid crystal television to a small mobile device.

You can select a variety of characters and backgrounds freely.

Then to enable heartfelt and lively communication totally depends on your thought about your friends and family members.
