Scheme of Pay Forward

2017-05-18

One of features that Cinematic Communication has is thinking about the feeling of your communication partner and there is a scheme to support this feature.

The scheme is Pay Forward. It makes character data that you own available free to your friends or family members.

Any members who are registered in your friend list can use character data that you own to communicate with you as long as you agree to let them use.

For example, the scheme will work to let you pay for character data that creators made for business including the usage of your friends and family members in advance.

On the other hand, your friends or family members cannot use your character data to communicate with others except you. They need to purchase the character data separately by themselves.

By using the scheme of Pay Forward everybody can enjoy Cinematic Communication immediately. For creators and distributors the scheme can help them to introduce characters that they made or produced broadly by the hands of users.
