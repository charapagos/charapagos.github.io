Make use of online storage

2017-05-18

Before starting Cinematic Communication, online storage is necessary to save and manage character data and exchanged messages.

By means of using online storage, you can enjoy Cinematic Communication anytime, anywhere and from any devices.

Not only users but also distributors do not need to set up their own server and provide their own online storage service.

Cinematic Communication itself does not provide its own online storage service too. Users and distributors by themselves need to pick up service that they usually use or other one that they can trust.

You can also save all data into a hard disc drive of your computer.
