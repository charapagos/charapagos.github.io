Emotional Engine

2017-05-18

Emotional Engine embedded in charapagos is the heart of Cinematic Communication and literally driving force to make characters express and act. To be more precise, it has a function to combine emotional words or phrases in sentences that users wrote with characters’ expression or action data. Combination between emotional words and characters’ expression or action data is up to users and everybody can do it easily. Once they write sentences normally, Emotional Engine will automatically find emotional words in them and select appropriate characters’ expression or action data along with that.

Multiply Filtering System

2017-05-18

Multiply Filtering System is built in Emotional Engine as its main function. The system is composed of multiple filters and they are put on top of each other. Grid shaped areas are set in each filter. Expression or action data for a character is located on each grid shaped area in a filter one by one. It is called a character filter. Words or phrases that show emotion or action are also located on each grid shaped area in other filter.

Combination of filters

2017-05-18

As its name suggests, Multiply Filtering System can combine many filters together. The basic scheme is to make data recorded on a first filter have priority over other data on lower filters. On the other hand, if there are no relevant words or phrases in data recorded on the first filter, then the system will try to find relevance in data recorded on the second, third or lower filters in turn.

Types of data to be registered on filters

2017-05-18

Two types of data, character data and words or phrases related to expression data are basic to be registered on filters. However, you can register a variety of data without them. For example, after you register temperature data from minus 20C until 50C in advance, you can combine a phrase that suggests more than 25C in a message with character data that express sweating. On the contrary when a phrase that suggests less than 10C is used, character data that express freezing can be combined with the phrase.

Scheme of Pay Forward

2017-05-18

One of features that Cinematic Communication has is thinking about the feeling of your communication partner and there is a scheme to support this feature. The scheme is Pay Forward. It makes character data that you own available free to your friends or family members.
