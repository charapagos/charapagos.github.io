Teenage Walkers Project

2017-05-18

One of developers for Cinematic Communication has experience to provide opportunities for teenagers who have different cultural backgrounds to know each other through online communication in US, Japan and Singapore as one of the first cases in the world. About Teenage Walkers Project Considering the scale of the activity and the educational consequence, Teenage Walkers Project implemented from 1997 until 1999 would be regarded as one of first successful cases utilizing ICT for cross-cultural communication program.
