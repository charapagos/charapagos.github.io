The right of character data

2017-05-18

As one of aims that Cinematic Communication has, it tries to promote creative activities and establish Win-Win relationships between creators and users shortening a distance between them. Regarding the right of character data, it also considers to establish Win-Win relationships between them.

It has a scheme to be highly convenient for both creators and users taking an approach from both technical and social sides.

Technical approach

Through the scheme of Pay Forward, your friends and family members registered in your friend list can freely use character data that you own to enjoy Cinematic Communication.

Even only one person starts Cinematic Communication, this kind of scheme enables others try it easily. We can expect that their communication network will expand instantly from that point. The scheme will prevent the illegal use of character data only for trying Cinematic Communication.

Through the scheme of Trust Alliance, as long as you exchange public keys that are necessary to encrypt a set of data for Cinematic Communication including character data with only your reliable partners, it enables to respect technically the right of character data that creators made.

Social approach

When creators sell their character data, a part of profit will be collected. It will be used for educational activities that aim to foster the next generation of creators and users.

Information on the collected profit will be opened to the public. It enables every user to make sure of how much each creator or distributer contributes to the educational activities whenever he/she wishes.

Through the combination of the technical approach with the social one, Cinematic Communication aims to provide an ideal environment where users can enjoy convenient communication as much as possible and will respect the right of character data in the form that creators can allow and they can find enough reasons to purchase character data from creators based on the premise that a part of profit will be used for fostering the next generation of creators and users.
