Three aims

2017-05-18

Cinematic Communication holds up three aims.

1. Cinematic Communication will propose a more positive, heartfelt and lively communication style.

These days we can see remarkable progress in both the hardware and software of mobile devices. The performance has almost achieved the same level of personal computer.

You cannot make use of the technical progress fully as long as you consume it only by downloading and watching video data or sending and receiving short messages.

Cinematic Communication will propose a communication style that makes the best use of the technical advantage.

2. Cinematic Communication will broaden more opportunities for creators to be active through shortening a distance between them and users.

It aims to increase opportunities for creators to learn what kinds of works or expression users prefer by enabling them to listen to usersâ€™ voice directly.

If creators can learn more about users, it will increase opportunities for them to make more ideas about new works or innovative expression methods.

Eventually users can expect more interesting works produced by them.

3. Through social approaches, Cinematic Communication will support to foster the next generation of creators and users simultaneously.

A part of profits that Cinematic Communication can yield will be returned to social approaches such as Carrier Educational Activities and UNESCO Salon Video Competition and to support to foster the next generation of creators and users.

How many the next generation of creators we can foster and furthermore how many selective users who can recognize the high value of creative activities and works we can foster simultaneously are essential to sustainable development including the growth of media/content industries in the world.
